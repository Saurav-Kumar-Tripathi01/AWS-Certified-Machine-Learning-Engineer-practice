## 🧠 Few Things to Say to Yourself

✅ I build before I perfect

## 🤝 Promise Me One Thing
Don’t use AI abruptly.
Yes, AI is advanced. Yes, we are literally preparing for AI. But AI can sometimes dump a lot of information — and when that happens, you don’t always get pushed into decision‑making.
And without decision‑making, learning becomes shallow.
Instead, do this first:

Use Google
Search directly in the browser for issues
Use Stack Overflow — it’s a good tool
Try to read as much documentation as possible
Reading documentation:

Builds understanding
Improves language
Improves the way you think



## 🧩 About Problems (This Is Important)
Found a problem?
Don’t keep staring at it. Break it into pieces.
You can’t expect yourself to eat a massive cube of sugar —
but you can break it into smaller sugar cubes and consume it easily.
Tried something and it failed? Didn’t get the result you expected?
Why are you sad?
Now you know one way not to approach the problem.
Someone once said:

I know I have failed a thousand times.
I may not know the solution at the beginning,
but I know a thousand ways on how not to do it.


That’s learning.
So buckle up, boss.
